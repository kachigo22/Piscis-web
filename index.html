About Us

Piscis is working to strengthen the security and quality of food and water that families eat or drink. In Zambia, fish accounts for 25.3% of an average household’s protein intake. By providing real-time data, we are helping farmers increase their yields and conduct their farming in a more efficient way.| In addition, understanding water quality is essential for successful aquaculture, and our team is committed to creating and deploying solutions in aquaculture that are sustainable for the environment and accessible to farmers across the region.

The device measures two main parameters: temperature, and acidity. Using an Internet of Things (IoT) solution, we are working with different fish farms in Lusaka to collect this data. This data is shared real-time through our app - allowing fish farmers to receive information around water quality to name a few, and during emergencies, our users are notified via SMS.
